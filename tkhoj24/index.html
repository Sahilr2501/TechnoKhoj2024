Events

TECHNICAL EVENTS

Bugs Spot

In Bug spot event, participants will be provided a question file (containing incomplete or incorrect program).Participant need to solve logical or syntactical error to rules and predefined output.

Read More

IT Quiz

In the IT Quiz event, The questions shall be in the form of multiple choice, True/False statements, Specific-answer questions etc. There is no negative marking for all questions. Participants must complete the quiz within the time.

Read More

Poster Presentation

In the Poster Presentation competition, the participant is to present a poster based on future IT trends, research ideas, innovations, applications, and general knowledge in the field of science & technology, mathematics, education or any society related areas through poster presentation.

Read More


Events

NON-TECHNICAL EVENTS

Snakes & Ladders

In the Snakes & Ladders event, the participants first play the entry round and then each participant puts their counter on the space that says 'start here'. Take it in turns to roll the dice. Move your counter forward by the number of spaces shown on the dice.

Read More

Minute In One

In the minute in one event, the participant is must complete the game within one minute. Rules and required things are provided by the Coordinators of particular events. Participants must follow the rules.

Read More

Fun Games

In the Fun Games event, the participant is must complete the game within time. There are many games in the fun games event. Rules and required things are provided by the Coordinators of particular events. Participants must follow the rules.
